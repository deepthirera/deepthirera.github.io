Vinod Kannan & Associates., Cost Accountant Firm advises entities relating to maintenance of books of accounts & filing of various returns under taxation laws. We provide advisory services for tax planning based on the nature of the business entities.

We facilitate individuals / entities for filing Income Tax return*, Tax Deducted at Source return, Goods and Service tax return, Registrar of Companies return, Professional tax return.

We also provide services relating to maintenance of cost records in accordance with generally accepted cost accounting principles (GACAP) and Cost Accounting Standards issued by Institute of Cost Accountants of India (Notified by Companies (Cost Records and Audit) Rules, 2014 on 30 June 2014.

  • Income Tax Returns (ITR)
  • Goods and Service Tax Return
  • Registrar of Companies (ROC) Return
  • Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) Return
  • Annual Information Return (AIR)
  • Books of accounts under Taxation Laws
  • Maintenance of Costing records for applicable companies
* ITRs except those which need to be signed by CAs.
